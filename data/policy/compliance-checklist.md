# Compliance Checklist — Mortgage Origination (US Federal)

> Used by Stage 6 compliance workers. Each item is a discrete check; the worker
> verifies and reports a pass/fail with rationale.

## TRID (Truth in Lending / RESPA Integrated Disclosure)

| # | Check | Source | Trigger |
| - | ----- | ------ | ------- |
| T1 | Loan Estimate (LE) delivered within 3 business days of application | 12 CFR 1026.19(e)(1)(iii) | Application complete |
| T2 | LE contains all required fields (loan terms, projected payments, closing cost summary) | Reg Z Appendix H | LE delivery |
| T3 | Intent to Proceed (ITP) signed before lender incurs fees | 12 CFR 1026.19(e)(2) | After LE |
| T4 | Closing Disclosure (CD) delivered ≥ 3 business days before consummation | 12 CFR 1026.19(f)(1)(ii) | Pre-close |
| T5 | If APR change > 0.125%, redisclosed CD + new 3-day wait | 12 CFR 1026.19(f)(2)(ii) | Material change |
| T6 | Zero-tolerance fees match LE exactly (origination, transfer taxes) | 12 CFR 1026.19(e)(3)(i) | CD prep |
| T7 | 10%-tolerance bucket fees within 10% cumulative variance | 12 CFR 1026.19(e)(3)(ii) | CD prep |

## HMDA (Home Mortgage Disclosure Act)

| # | Check | Source | Trigger |
| - | ----- | ------ | ------- |
| H1 | Government monitoring info collected (race, ethnicity, sex) | 12 CFR 1003.4(a)(10) | Application |
| H2 | "Information not provided" allowed if applicant declines | 12 CFR 1003.4(a)(10)(ii) | Application |
| H3 | Income, loan amount, property location reported | 12 CFR 1003.4(a) | Application |
| H4 | Loan reportable to CFPB / FFIEC by March 1 following year | 12 CFR 1003.5(a) | Annual |
| H5 | If denied: denial reason coded (1-9 per Reg C) | 12 CFR 1003.4(a)(16) | Decision |

## ECOA / Regulation B (Equal Credit Opportunity Act)

| # | Check | Source | Trigger |
| - | ----- | ------ | ------- |
| E1 | No prohibited basis used in decision (race, sex, religion, marital, age, national origin, public assistance income) | 15 USC 1691(a) | Decision |
| E2 | If denied: Adverse Action Notice within 30 days, with specific reasons | 15 USC 1691(d) / 12 CFR 1002.9 | Denial |
| E3 | Right to receive appraisal copy disclosed | 12 CFR 1002.14 | Application |
| E4 | If joint applicants: both notices addressed appropriately | 12 CFR 1002.9(f) | Joint app |

## QM / ATR (Qualified Mortgage / Ability-to-Repay)

| # | Check | Source | Trigger |
| - | ----- | ------ | ------- |
| Q1 | DTI ≤ 43% (or AUS approval = Appendix Q exempt) | 12 CFR 1026.43(e)(2)(vi) | Underwriting |
| Q2 | Income verified per Appendix Q (W-2, 1040, transcripts) | 12 CFR 1026.43(c)(2)(i) | Underwriting |
| Q3 | No negative amortization | 12 CFR 1026.43(e)(2)(i) | Loan terms |
| Q4 | No interest-only payments | 12 CFR 1026.43(e)(2)(ii) | Loan terms |
| Q5 | No balloon payment (unless small creditor exception) | 12 CFR 1026.43(e)(2)(iii) | Loan terms |
| Q6 | Term ≤ 30 years | 12 CFR 1026.43(e)(2)(iv) | Loan terms |
| Q7 | Points and fees ≤ 3% of loan amount (for loans ≥ $100K) | 12 CFR 1026.43(e)(3)(i) | Closing |

## OFAC (Treasury Sanctions Screening)

| # | Check | Source | Trigger |
| - | ----- | ------ | ------- |

| O2 | Seller(s) screened | 31 CFR 501 | Pre-close |
