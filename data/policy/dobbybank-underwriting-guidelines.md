# DobbyBank, N.A. — Residential Mortgage Underwriting Guidelines (Excerpt)

> **Internal use only.** This excerpt aligns with Fannie Mae Selling Guide and
> Freddie Mac Single-Family Seller/Servicer Guide as of effective date.
> Effective: 2026-Q1.

## 1. Conventional Conforming Loan Limits

- 1-unit standard: $766,550 (2026)
- 1-unit high-cost area (Alameda County, CA): $1,149,825 (2026)
- 1-unit Castro Valley (94552): qualifies as high-cost — **conforming up to $1,149,825**

## 2. Credit Score Thresholds

| Score (mid of tri-merge) | Treatment                                  |
| ------------------------ | ------------------------------------------ |
| ≥ 740                    | Best pricing tier (LLPA grid floor)        |
| 720–739                  | Standard pricing                           |
| 680–719                  | LLPA add-on; pricing penalty               |
| 620–679                  | Manual underwriter review required         |
| < 620                    | DECLINE (sub-prime; refer to FHA channel) |

## 3. Loan-to-Value (LTV) Thresholds

| LTV     | PMI Required | Other                                     |
| ------- | ------------ | ----------------------------------------- |
| ≤ 80%   | No           | Borrower-paid PMI not required            |
| 80.01–89.99% | Yes (LPMI or borrower-paid monthly) | Requires reserves ≥ 6 mo PITI |
| 90–95%  | Yes (BPMI required)                        | Manual review                |
| > 95%   | DECLINE (refer to FHA / VA / USDA channel) | Out of conventional box     |

## 4. Debt-to-Income (DTI) Thresholds (Qualified Mortgage)

- **Front-end DTI** (PITI ÷ qualifying income): target ≤ 28%, max 36%
- **Back-end DTI** (PITI + recurring debts ÷ qualifying income):
  - ≤ 43% → AUS-approved (Dodd-Frank QM safe harbor)
  - 43.01–45% → Manual review with compensating factors required
  - \> 45% → DECLINE

## 5. Reserves Requirements (post-close liquid assets)

| Property Type     | Months PITI Reserves |
| ----------------- | -------------------- |
| Primary residence (SFR) | 2 mo            |
| Primary residence (condo, HOA) | 2 mo     |
| Second home       | 6 mo                 |
| Investment property | 6 mo               |

## 6. Income Documentation (W-2 Borrower)

- **Years of history required:** 2 (24-month average)
- **Documents required:**
  - W-2s for prior 2 years (paper or electronic)
  - Pay stubs covering most recent 30 days
  - **IRS Form 4506-C signed** at application + transcript pulled before close
- **VOE:** written VOE OR closing-day verbal VOE (Fannie B3-3.1-07)

## 7. Asset Documentation

- **Bank statements:** Most recent 2 consecutive months from all accounts being used for down payment + reserves
- **Large undocumented deposits:** Any deposit >50% of borrower's monthly income must be sourced (gift letter, sale, etc.). AML/BSA hygiene.
- **Retirement assets:** Counted at 60% of vested balance (haircut for tax + 10% early withdrawal penalty)
- **Brokerage assets:** Counted at 70-100% depending on volatility profile
- **Gift funds:** Permitted with signed gift letter + donor's source-of-funds documentation. Cannot be loan.

## 8. Property — Appraisal Requirements

- **Form 1004** (URAR) for SFR or condo; PUD = Form 1004C
- **Comp Sales:** 3 closed sales within 12 months, within 1-mile radius (urban)
- **Reconciliation:** Sales comparison primary; cost approach secondary
- **Appraised value vs contract:** If appraised < contract → DECLINE or renegotiate price (lender lends against the LESSER of appraised or contract)

## 9. Manual Underwriter Compensating Factors

When a loan misses one threshold (e.g., DTI 44.5%), the underwriter MAY approve with documented compensating factors:

- Reserves ≥ 6 months PITI (vs minimum 2)
- Down payment ≥ 25% (vs minimum 20)
- Stable 5+ year employment with same employer
- Documented history of meeting equivalent housing obligation (rent ≥ proposed PITI)

## 10. Required Compliance Gates Before Funding

1. **TRID** — Loan Estimate (LE) delivered within 3 business days of application
2. **TRID** — Closing Disclosure (CD) delivered ≥ 3 business days before closing
3. **TRID** — Fee tolerance check (zero / 10% / unlimited buckets)
4. **HMDA** — Government monitoring fields collected (Form 1003 Section 7)
5. **ECOA / Reg B** — If denied: Adverse Action Notice within 30 days, with specific reasons
6. **QM / ATR** — DTI verified ≤ 43%, income documented per §6, no risky features (no negative amortization, no IO, term ≤ 30 yr)
7. **OFAC** — Sanctions screening of borrower(s), seller(s), guarantor(s)
8. **AML / BSA** — Source of funds verified; SAR filed if applicable
